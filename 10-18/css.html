Mount Everest
Geographical Marvel: Mount Everest, known as "Sagarmatha" in Nepal and "Chomolungma" in Tibet, rises to an astounding height of 29,032 feet (8,849 meters) above sea level. It is a part of the Mahalangur Range, which stretches across the Nepal-Tibet border. The summit of Everest is enveloped by clouds, snow, and a biting cold that challenge even the bravest souls.
